📖 How to Play

Simple rules for maximum fun

1

Add Players

Enter 2–10 player names in the setup screen. The more, the merrier!

2

Pick a Mode

Select your difficulty and category preferences before starting. Choose what fits your group.

3

Your Turn

When it's your turn, choose Truth (answer a question honestly) or Dare (complete a challenge).

4

No Chickening Out

If you refuse a Truth or can't complete a Dare, you get a penalty decided by the group. Be brave!

5

Skip Wisely

Each player can skip once per round. Use it wisely — you only get one free pass!

6

Keep It Fun!

Always respect others. Keep questions and dares light-hearted and appropriate for all players.
